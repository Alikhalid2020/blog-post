Let's talk about Mental Health!

What do you really mean when you say “I’m fine”?

I’m not currently in therapy, but I think if I was, a therapist would help me work through my dangerous habit of over-saying “I’m fine.” Sure, it’s an OK response in the checkout line of a grocery store. It’s also valid in large settings or groups where we don’t feel comfortable letting our walls down. But I tend slather it all over one-on-one conversations with people I care about, as well as during doctor’s appointments where I’m the direct opposite of decent.

When I say "I'm fine", I tend to mean:

  • I'm not doing well.
  • I'm numb.
  • My life is not what I want it to be but I'm still here.

What about you? If you’re comfortable, feel free to use the comments section of this post to say how you truly are and let it be a small step in the right direction of healing.
